About the summit

The Mindanao Student Chapter of the Philippine Institute of Industrial Engineers links IE student organizations in the region. The summit serves as a workspace for councils to set common goals and align activities.

Delegates share quick updates on membership, events, academics, and student concerns from their campus. This gives a clear view of how councils are doing and where help is needed.

Activities use short inputs and small group work. Schools go home with a regional calendar, contact persons in other councils, and clear follow up tasks.
Summit goals
  • Agree on a regional IE student calendar
  • Clarify shared academic and student issues
  • Form groups to lead joint projects
Typical delegates
  • Council presidents and core officers
  • Project and events heads
  • Finance, logistics, and external officers
